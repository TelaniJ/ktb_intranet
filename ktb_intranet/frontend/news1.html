Kenya Receives Prestigious Tourism Award

June 23, 2024

Kenya has been honored with the prestigious "Best Safari Destination" award at the World Travel Awards 2024. The award ceremony took place in Dubai, where representatives from the Kenya Tourism Board were present to receive the accolade.

This recognition highlights Kenya's commitment to providing exceptional safari experiences, showcasing the country's rich wildlife, stunning landscapes, and dedication to conservation.

In his acceptance speech, the CEO of the Kenya Tourism Board expressed gratitude to the international community for recognizing Kenya's efforts in promoting sustainable tourism. He also acknowledged the hard work of the local communities and stakeholders who play a crucial role in maintaining the country's tourism standards.

Kenya continues to attract tourists from around the world, offering a unique blend of adventure, culture, and natural beauty. This award serves as a testament to the country's unwavering dedication to enhancing the tourism experience for all visitors.
