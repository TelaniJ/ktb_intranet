KTB MENTORSHIP

Boost your professional development. Empower and guide fellow colleagues

The KTB mentorship program is a flexible and open platform designed to match aspiring and developing professionals to the experience and knowledge of a mentor that suits them best. It is open to all staff, at all levels across the organization. We know that working in a destination marketing organization can get very busy and that’s why the mentoring program is designed to work around you. Whether mentor or mentee, it’s your decision as to how you wish for your relationship to work. You can familiarize yourself on what mentoring is all about through the KTB Mentoring Guide and the respective resources available on the intranet which will be updated periodically. Registration forms are available on the intra-net. Once you’ve filled out the form, send it through to HR who will then advice on the next steps.

Mentees

Registering as a mentee will give you access to mentors who are experienced and specialized in different aspects of personal and professional development. Take the opportunity to shorten your learning curve and enhance your career development in the process. KTB has very experienced, qualified and well exposed staff to local and international environments, cultures, business environments etc. It’s not just for junior staff, either. Our mentors vary from across the organization with diverse experiences and expertise to share. Guidance is available throughout the process. We’ve got your back. So dive in and leverage the benefits of mentoring.

Mentors

As a mentor, you get to give back to the KTB community & beyond by helping shape the career and future of others. By paying forward, you’ll receive recognition as an expert in your field and develop your personal leadership and coaching skills. It’s also a fantastic way to expose yourself to fresh perspectives and ideas.
